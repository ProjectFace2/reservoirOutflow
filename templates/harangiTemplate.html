Harangi

Harangi River and Reservior

Harangi river is one of the tributaries of Cauvery. This river originates at Pushpagiri Hills of Western Ghats.The estimate length of the river is approximately 50 Kms. It joins River Cauvery at Somwarpet Taluk.

The reservoir for this river is built in Somwarpet Taluk called as Harangi Reservoir. The catchment areas for this reservoir covers all three taluks namely Somwarpet, Madikeri and Virajpet, which counts upto 58.5 Sq Km. Inflow into the reservoir is the consequence of the downpour in the catchment areas.

The height of this reservoir is approximately 2,859 ft and its capacity is 8.07 TMC (93,402.77 Cusecs). Generally the reservoir is fed, during the South West monsoon periods(i.e between June and November). The Annual Mean Discharge(i.e the outflow) is 1,130 million M.㎥
